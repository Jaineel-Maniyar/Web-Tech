About University

PDEU was established as a Petroleum University in 2007 with a focus on fossil fuels and later emerged as India's only energy university in the year 2021, dedicated to energy education and research. Today, the university has marked its presence as a renowned institution with noteworthy national and international visibility. PDEU is aggresively contributing towards Nation's AatmaNirbharBharat Abhiyan.Pandit Deendayal Energy University’s 100-acre campus is located in Gandhinagar, the capital city of Gujarat, situated 23 km north of Ahmedabad. The city is renowned for its cultural richness and vibrant social life.

PDEU offers a wide range of courses in engineering, arts, and management, providing students with maximum exposure and opportunities through various national and international exchange programs with top universities worldwide. The university is also committed to the development of its faculty and staff through joint exchange and research programs.

Academics

School of Technology

SoT was established with the vision of being the leading centre of innovation and learning in the emerging areas of Industry 4.0. It offers B.Tech. in the fields of Civil, Mechanical, Computer, Electronics & Communication Engineering & ICT besides the M.Tech. and Ph.D. Programs in the above areas. It also offers B.Sc.(Hons.), M.Sc. and Ph.D. in Mathematics. SoT has received more than Rs. 200 Crore for Centres of Excellence Projects and 60 ongoing projects being sponsored by government and private entities. The School is contributing to the AatmanirbharBharat Initiatives through its Centers of Excellence, including the recent addition of Metal 3D printing facility dedicated to the nation for research in the Energy, Healthcare, Automotive, and Aerospace Sector. It has other Centres in the areas of Automation, Automobiles and Next Generation Computing (250,000 USD).

School of Petroleum Technology

The school is playing a pivotal role in preparing Energy Ambassadors for the Nation. It offers B. Tech., M. Tech. & Ph.D. programs in Energy-related domains i.e. Petroleum, Chemical, Electrical, Biotechnology, Solar & Nuclear Technology. It also offers B.Sc. (Hons.), M.Sc. & Ph.D. in Physics and Chemistry. With an effective hand-holding with the Government of Gujarat and the Industry, SoET has set up Centers of Excellence in • Solar Research & Development and a Grid connected 1.7 MW Solar farm • Solar PV Panel assembly line of 45 MW capacity, the first skill India Initiative in the country • Energy storage, Redox flow with 25 years shelf life, scalable for 1 MW and above • Sustainable technologies (Green H2, Methanol, Carbon Capture, Biofuel 1.5M USD) • Centre for Drilling Cementing & Stimulation • Centre for Biofuel & Bio-Energy Studies (CBBS).

School of Petroleum Management

School of Management (formerly School of Petroleum Management) is among Top 100 Management Institutes as per NIRF Rankings. It offers two-year full-time residential MBA programmes in Energy and Infrastructure Management and General Management with specialization in Marketing, Finance, Human Resources, Operations Management and minor in Business Analytics. In addition, SoM offers Executive MBA for working executives in hybrid mode, Ph.D. programme (full-time and part-time) and Management Development Programmes. This trimester system MBA programme includes eight weeks of compulsory summer training for the students. It also includes field visits, independent study courses and constant interaction with industry personnel to prepare industry ready professionals. It has Business Analytics Teaching Lab which provides students with knowledge and skills that empower them in tackling business problems using appropriate data analytics. It boasts of 100% placements for last 4 consecutive years with top notch companies visiting for campus placements.

School of Liberal Studies

The school has a transdisciplinary academic program and a four-year undergraduate structure, which aligns with India's New National Education Policy (NEP). The four-year structure in BA, BBA and B.Com. allows the students to explore their strengths and be problem solvers with an array of solutions countering critical aspects. The courses are clustered in Social Sciences, Administration, Marketing, Communications and Literature. The Audit Courses are envisioned to impart value based education and develop a skill set amongst its students to broaden their vision. SLS also offers Master's programs in Public Administration, Politics and International Relations, English, Economics, Mass Communication, Psychology and Master of Commerce. The school also offers Ph.D. programs in Humanities, Arts, Social Sciences, Business Administration, and Commerce.
